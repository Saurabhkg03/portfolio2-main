CNC

The CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machine is a powerful tool for automated machining operations. It utilizes computer-controlled movements to create intricate designs and precise cuts on various materials, such as wood, plastic, and metal. Here are the key components of the CNC machine:

- Control Panel: The interface for inputting commands and controlling the machine's operations.
- Spindle: The cutting tool attached to the machine that rotates at high speeds.
- Worktable: The platform where the material is secured for machining.
- Axis System: The X, Y, and Z axes that determine the movement of the cutting tool.

Operating a CNC machine requires a combination of technical knowledge, creativity, and careful attention to detail. By following this documentation and practicing safe and precise machining, you can create intricate and accurate parts for a wide range of applications.
